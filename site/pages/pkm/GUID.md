## Tips

### Markdown Guidelines

- Using HTML
- Two spaces or an empty line
- Maximum of 6 `#` for leveling
- **Bold** or __Bold__
- ***Italics*** or *Italics*
- ==Highlight== or <mark>highlight</mark>
- :smile: or copy
- `code` or 
- Link: https://www.pirahansiah.com
- Blockquotes: > or >> or >>> ...
- Horizontal rule: ***
- [x] todo
- Tab table

### Markdown Metadata Frontmatter

- YAML
- Add fence to both sides: --- ---

- Foam: Show graph
